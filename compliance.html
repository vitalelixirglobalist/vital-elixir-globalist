1. Licensing & Trade Identification

At Vital Elixir Globalist, we maintain the mandatory credentials required for international pharmaceutical trade originating from India.

  • Importer-Exporter Code (IEC) issued by the Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT).
  • Active GST registration with compliant use of Letters of Undertaking (LUT) or bonds for export taxation requirements.
  • Membership with Pharmexcil (Pharmaceutical Export Promotion Council of India) or an equivalent industry body to align with sector-specific export norms.

2. Export Regulatory Compliance

  • Full adherence to the Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA) 1999 for foreign remittances and export income realisation.
  • Strict compliance with DGFT export policy guidelines for pharmaceutical goods, including SCOMET classifications and export licensing.
  • Approvals obtained from Central Drugs Standard Control Organisation (CDSCO) and relevant state drug authorities to guarantee safety, quality, and regulatory alignment.
