About Our Process

A transparent and reliable workflow for international medicine supply

Step 1

Product Discussion & Quotation

  • Our team reviews your medication requirements in detail
  • Complete product information and specifications are shared
  • Transparent quotation aligned with order volume and destination country

Step 2

Order Confirmation & Secure Payment

  • A secure payment link is shared once quantities are confirmed
  • Payment confirmation triggers immediate issuance of an official invoice

Step 3

Order Processing & Secure Packaging (Within 24 Hours)

  • Medicine images are shared before dispatch
  • Packed parcel images include recipient name, shipping address, and tracking ID

Step 4

Shipment & Live Tracking

  • Orders ship in shock-proof, export-grade packaging
  • Live tracking ID is provided with proactive shipment updates
  • Track directly on the official USPS website: usps.com
