How long does it take for the parcel to get delivered? +

Most orders are delivered within 8–10 days, and in rare cases may take up to a maximum of two weeks.

What happens if the parcel is stuck in customs? +

In the rare event that a parcel shows no movement for 10–15 days, we arrange a reimbursement parcel to ensure you receive your medication without additional stress.

Customer satisfaction and delivery assurance are our priority.

How can I verify the expiry date of the medication before delivery? +

Before dispatch, we share clear and detailed images of the medication, including:

  • Product packaging details
  • Recipient name and delivery address on the parcel

You can cross-verify all details before shipment, and our team is always available to reconfirm if needed.
